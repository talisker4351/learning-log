### 2025-01-11 | Feature Selection
**Today**  
I learned that features should be selected based on their relevance to the prediction, not by using all available variables.

**Insight**  
By choosing only variables that explain future outcomes, the model becomes simpler and more accurate.

**Connection**  
This approach can be applied to sales and inventory data to focus on key factors like demand and price, leading to better ordering decisions.
